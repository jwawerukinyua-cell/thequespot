Why The Que Spot

This Is Not Your
Average Pool Hall.

Every pool hall in Kahawa West charges per game in a loud, alcohol-driven environment. We are building something completely different — a calm, premium, members-only sanctuary where the game is taken seriously and the community reflects that.

Fully Automated Tables

Our pool tables are fully automated with M-Pesa integration. Pay via M-Pesa, play your session. No cash, no queues, no disputes.

Skill-First Environment

Every session is structured around improvement. Whether beginner or seasoned player, The Que Spot is built for those who take the game seriously.

100% Sober Sanctuary

No alcohol, no drugs, no miraa, no cigarettes. A genuinely safe, calm environment built for focus and community, not distraction.

Safe Networking Community

More than a game — a space where meaningful connections are built. From serious competitors to complete beginners, everyone has a place here.

📱

M-Pesa Powered. Fully Automated.

Our automated tables are integrated with M-Pesa — Kenya's most trusted payment platform. Book your session, pay instantly, play without hassle.
